About Me

Almost everything is going to happen for you automatically - you don't have to spend any time working or worrying. Mix your color marbly don't mix it dead. It's a good way of getting rid of all your anxieties and hostilities. Isn't that fantastic that you can create an almighty tree that fast? I was blessed with a very steady hand; and it comes in very handy when you're doing these little delicate things. We have no limits to our world. We're only limited by our imagination. We'll make some happy little bushes here.

A fan brush is a fantastic piece of equipment. Use it. Make friends with it. There's not a thing in the world wrong with washing your brush. I will take some magic white, and a little bit of Vandyke brown and a little touch of yellow. Put your feelings into it, your heart, it's your world. See. We take the corner of the brush and let it play back-and-forth. If you hypnotize it, it will go away. Put light against light - you have nothing. Put dark against dark - you have nothing. It's the contrast of light and dark that each give the other one meaning. We don't make mistakes we just have happy little accidents. Here's something that's fun.
